The Criminal Section is responsible for enforcing the nation's federal criminal civil rights statutes. Much of the Section's enforcement activity involves the investigation and prosecution of violent, bias-motivated crimes, as well as deprivations of rights by individuals acting under color of law, to include law enforcement officers and government officials.
