Field guide for report routing (version - June 27, 2020)

This guide is intended to help new intake specialists.
This is an internal guide designed to help new intake specialists know when to redirect violations reports to other sections.

Why is it important to reroute reports correctly & quickly?
Because we want to give the section best-positioned to analyze any given report the chance to do so as soon as possible.

Questions?

If you have a question about whether you should route a report to a particular section, start by talking to your supervisor

If you still have questions, call or message the current point of contact for a given section.

Final Note - This guide is designed to be practical, not precise. The federal civil rights laws we enforce are varied and complex. When we list a section for routing, it does not mean that the section will necessarily have enforcement authority for that report. It also does not mean this section is the only one who might have enforcement authority. Being listed here only means this section is likely best positioned to know the right next step for this kind of report.
