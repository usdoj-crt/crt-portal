Access to the Civil Rights Division's Reporting Portal is restricted to authorized users only.

To obtain access:

  1. Locate your section's access POC in the chart below and email them requesting access to the portal
  2. Your section POC should send an email to CRTHelpDesk@usdoj.gov authorizing your access to the application
  3. The helpdesk will create a ticket and work with the portal's admins to grant you access
  4. Portal admins will contact you once access has been granted (usually within 24 hours)
