Search examples

Here are some examples of using AND, OR, minus sign (-), “quotation marks”, and (parentheses) to search keywords in Personal Description:

Mask AND Vaccine: Gets results for reports with both words listed.
Mask OR Vaccine: Gets results for reports with either of the words listed.
Mask AND Vaccine OR Shot: Gets results for reports that include Mask and Vaccine and all reports that include Shot.
Mask -Vaccine: Gets results for report that include Mask and exclude Vaccine because of the minus signs (-).
“Mask Vaccine”: Gets results for reports with similar matching words in the quotation marks*.
Mask AND (Vaccine OR Shot): Gets results that have both Mark and Vaccine plus also reports with both Mask and Shot*.
^Mask Vaccine$: Look only for records exactly matching "Mask Vaccine".

*Disclaimer: Do not use (parentheses) and “quotation marks” in the same search. Results may be unexpected.
